Tarantulas

Introduction

Tarantulas are spiders but bigger. The biggest tarantula has a body that is 4.75 inches long with an 11 inch leg span. Those tarantulas are Goliath bird eating tarantulas. Currently there are 1,040 species of tarantula that have been identified. Tarantulas are also nocturnal

General Information

Tarantula babies are commonly referred to as slings. They are under 2 inches big and can start out at under a 1/2 inch. Tarantulas typically eat crickets and mealworms, especially in captivity, but eat many other prey as well. They only need to eat 1 or twice every week or two and can go a whole year without eating and still be alive as long as they are kept well hydrated.

How do they move?

The way that tarantulas legs work is almost completely opposite of how our muscles work. Their legs start out contracted and they use their hemolmphs (their blood equivalent) to stretch out their legs (hydraulics). This is why when tarantulas (and other spiders) die they die in a tight curl instead of limp and flat.

Molting

Depending on what point of their life they are in, tarantulas do a lot of molting. While they are young they molt around once a month, while older tarantulas molt every year or two. However, only female tarantulas can molt after maturity, male tarantulas die before they can molt after maturity.

New World vs. Old World

New world tarantulas are from the western hemisphere and old world are from the eastern hemisphere. Old world and new world tarantulas have a lot of differences in behavior and physique. Old word are fast, less hair, and tend to be more aggressive, and with worse venom. New world are a lot slower, more hairy, and have venom comparable to a bee sting. New world tarantulas have hairs called urticating hairs, which is a defense mechanism where they kick hairs into the air that cause irritation to potential predators.

TLDR:

New World Traits:

  • From the Western Hemisphere
  • Slower
  • More Hairy
  • Weaker Venom
  • Urticating Hairs

Old World Traits:

  • From the Eastern Hemisphere
  • Faster
  • Less Hair
  • Stronger Venom
  • No Urticating Hairs
